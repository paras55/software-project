Features

SOCIAL DISTANCING DETECTOR

Social distancing is a method used to control the spread of contagious diseases. As the name suggests, social distancing implies that people should physically distance themselves from one another, reducing close contact, and thereby reducing the spread of a contagious disease (such as coronavirus) So our tool will detect whether the same is being followed or not.

MASK DETECTION

Wearing mask is a must during this pandemic situation, So our tool will contribute to the same by detecting whether a person is wearing a mask or not.
